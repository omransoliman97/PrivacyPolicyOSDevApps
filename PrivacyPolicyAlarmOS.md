# AlarmOS Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-08

AlarmOS ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app processes and why.

## Data We Process

### 1) Alarm and App Data (stored on your device)

AlarmOS stores the following locally on your device:

- Alarm settings (time, label, repeat days, ringtone, vibration)
- Presets and generated alarms
- Missed alarm log entries
- App settings (theme, snooze defaults, reminder settings)

This data is used only to provide core app functionality.

### 2) Notifications and Background Processing

AlarmOS requests notification and background-related permissions to:

- Trigger alarms and reminders
- Display missed/battery warning notifications
- Improve alarm reliability

### 3) Firebase Services

AlarmOS integrates Firebase for analytics and messaging:

- **Firebase Analytics**: anonymous usage events (for feature/performance improvement)
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)**: message token and push delivery metadata

Firebase may process device and app identifiers according to Google's privacy terms.

### 4) Advertising (Android)

AlarmOS uses Google AdMob banner ads on Android. AdMob may collect:

- Device/app identifiers
- Ad interaction and performance data
- Approximate diagnostics and fraud-prevention signals

Ad data processing is governed by Google's policies.

## What We Do Not Collect Directly

AlarmOS does not require account creation and does not directly collect:

- Name
- Email address
- Password
- Contacts
- Precise location

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by third-party service providers used by the app:

- Google Firebase
- Google AdMob

## Data Retention and Control

- Most app data is stored locally and remains until you delete it or uninstall the app.
- You can clear alarm logs and reset app data from inside the app.
- You can uninstall the app at any time to remove local data.

## Children

AlarmOS is not directed to children under 13.

## Security

We apply reasonable technical measures, but no system is 100% secure.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Disable notifications in device settings
- Revoke app permissions
- Limit ad personalization through Google settings (where available)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The updated version will include a new effective date.
