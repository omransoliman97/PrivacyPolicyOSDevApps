# Privacy Policy for Simple Tasks

**Last updated: July 18, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the Simple Tasks mobile application ("the App," "we," "us") handles information when you use it. Simple Tasks is developed by osdev.

## Overview

Simple Tasks is designed to work fully offline. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal information or task data. Everything you create in the App — tasks, notes, folders, and custom fields — stays on your device.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

- **No account or sign-up.** The App does not require or offer any kind of account, login, or user profile.
- **No data collection by us.** We do not operate any server that the App sends your tasks, notes, or settings to. There is no analytics SDK, no crash reporter, and no advertising SDK built into the App.
- **No tracking.** The App does not track your activity, location, or usage for advertising or profiling purposes.

## How Your Data Is Stored

All data you create in the App — tasks, notes, folders, custom fields, and app settings such as theme and accent color — is stored locally on your device only, using standard on-device storage. This data is never transmitted anywhere by the App.

## Backup, Export, and Import

The App includes an optional Export feature that lets you save all of your data as a single JSON file, and an Import feature that lets you load a previously exported file back in. These actions are entirely user-initiated:

- When you export, you choose where the file is saved using your device's own file picker.
- When you import, you choose which file to read using the same file picker.
- We do not receive a copy of this file, and it is never uploaded anywhere by the App. What you do with the exported file afterward (for example, backing it up to your own cloud storage) is entirely up to you and is governed by that service's own privacy policy, not ours.

## Internet Permission

The App requests the Internet permission for one purpose only: to download and cache the "Inter" typeface from Google Fonts the first time the App runs. This is a one-time font download, not used for analytics, advertising, or tracking, and no task data is sent as part of it.

## Third-Party Services

Simple Tasks does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or data-sharing services. The only external service the App communicates with is the Google Fonts font-delivery service described above.

## Children's Privacy

Simple Tasks is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, regardless of age, because the App does not collect personal information from any user.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are always in full control of it. You can delete any task, folder, or field directly within the App (including permanently, via the Trash), or remove all App data at once by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage from your device settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect new features. Changes will be posted at this same location with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
